Health

If the rabbits seems small and cute does not mean that they are easy to take care off. Rather as a dog as well as a rabbit owner i personally find raising rabbits more difficult. Rabbits are very delicate animals and they can get sick easily. If you feed something unhealthy or outside their necessary diet, they get sick and starts eating their own poop. Most of the deaths in rabbits are caused by digestive problems after they are fed human food too much. Rabbits have a very sensitive digestive system and they need a specific diet to stay healthy. One must wonder if they do not drink water at all after seeing them not drinking for days. Well this is because of bad diet. I would advice mixing some corrider or other types of green leaves or grass which they love to eat. The packaged "food" for rabbits is not really good for them and it can cause more harm than good. They are mostly made of hay and stuff but should be fed to them only once in a while.

Basic Knowledge

Rabbits are small gentle, and fluffy balls of lovely fur. They are herbivores and they eat hay, grass, and leafy greens. They actuallu sleeps with their eyes open most of the time. I have seen my rabbit sleeping while laying down and eyes clothes only when their is no one else in my room except me. They actually have a very good vision and they can see in almost 360 degrees i think. But i personally think that my rabbit is partially blind .If you look closely, they are always sniffingv here and there. This is because they have a very good sense of smell and also to communicate with other rabbits. They get lonely if they are left alone for too long. In most of the cases i would recommend rasing two rabbits together . My rabbits actually hates any rabbits and prefers to be with humans rather than its own species. They are very social animals and they need a lot of attention and lovr from their owners.

Precautions

As i said provide them with water regularly and make sure they are eating the right food which matches their balanced diet. Human food sometimes result in direct death. Wiping with wet tissue and small damp cloth is more than enough and adviced. My greatest mistake was direting my mother as i saw on videos. Rabbits are very scared of water and they can get really stressed out if they get wet. Do not pick them up by their ears or legs. They have very fragile and delicate bones.
